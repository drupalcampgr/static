About the Event

DrupalCamp Greece is back!
Bigger! Better!

DrupalCamp Greece is a conference that brings together web developers, designers, site-builders, content administrators and simple curious minds to learn, network and discuss about Drupal, Design, Development and Cutting Edge Web Technologies.

Drupal is strong because its community is stronger. With the release of Drupal 8 there is so much to hear and talk about that you’ll need lots of notes… Come for the code, stay for the community!

Featured Speakers

  • Morten DK Drupal 8 theming, Classy theme maintainer, Metal dude, Miniature wargaming. Proud Dad of Freya & English Bulldog owner
